Troubleshooting

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Make sure Tor is running on your system
  3. Verify the Tor control port (default: 9051) is accessible
  4. Check if your firewall is blocking the connection
  5. Try restarting the Tor service

Tor can be slower than regular internet due to its design for privacy. Here are some tips:

  • Try connecting to a different entry/exit node
  • Use bridges if you're in a country that censors Tor
  • Disable bandwidth-intensive features
  • Check if your internet connection is stable
